## Answer Key and Explanations

### Q1
**Answer:** B - Policies define the "what," while procedures document the "how."

### Q2
**Answer:** C - Accepting risk is appropriate when mitigation costs exceed expected impact.

### Q3
**Answer:** B - Vendor due diligence and contractual controls manage third-party risk.

### Q4
**Answer:** B - Awareness training reduces human-driven incidents by improving behavior.

### Q5
**Answer:** B - Classification dictates how data must be handled and protected.

### Q6
**Answer:** A - Change management enforces review, approval, and accountability to reduce unintended risk.

### Q7
**Answer:** B - Audits confirm whether controls exist and operate effectively.

### Q8
**Answer:** B - Legal and designated leadership/comms must control external messaging.

### Q9
**Answer:** B - A Business Impact Analysis identifies critical processes and recovery priorities.

### Q10
**Answer:** A - Lower MTTD shows the program is spotting incidents faster.
